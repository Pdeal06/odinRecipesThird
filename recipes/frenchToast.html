French Toast Sticks

Description

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these French toast sticks make a delicious breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 slices day-old bread, cut into thirds
  • 1 teaspoon confectioners' sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, milk, butter, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
  2. Line an air fryer basket with parchment paper.
  3. Dip each piece of bread into the milk mixture and place in the basket. Make sure they are not touching; cook in batches if necessary.
  4. Preheat the air fryer to 370 degrees F (188 degrees C).
  5. Add basket and cook bread for 6 minutes; flip and cook for 3 minutes more
  6. Sprinkle each stick with confectioners' sugar.
